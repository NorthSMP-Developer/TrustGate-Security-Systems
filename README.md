# TrustGate Security Systems

TrustGate is a free software developed and owned by NorthSMP. Setup and support videos will be made available to the public at a later date. Please note that this software is currently in beta version, and we are not responsible for any damage to your device. It is important to keep your password and/or PIN in a secure location where you can easily access it, as there is currently no option for password reset.

## Installation

During installation, customize the software according to your preferences.

## Setup

During the setup process, enter your desired password and then confirm it. Please note that the password must be at least 8 characters long and include at least one lowercase letter, one uppercase letter, and one special character. 

If you choose to add a PIN for additional security, enter your desired PIN and confirm it. Once you've completed the setup wizard, you will be prompted to enter your password and/or PIN. You can access the settings page at any time while the program is running. After confirming your password and/or PIN, you will gain access to more customization options. These include, but are not limited to, lock timeout, failed attempts, screen timeout, enabling/disabling the PIN, and changing your current password.
